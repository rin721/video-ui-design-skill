# Community Pattern

## Structure

```yaml
community:
  community_hero:
    purpose: "state shared purpose and participation value"
  value_statement:
    purpose: "explain who belongs and what they can do"
  discussion_highlights:
    purpose: "show active topics, questions, or updates"
  member_proof:
    purpose: "show participation, trust, and momentum"
  join_cta:
    purpose: "invite membership or contribution"
  footer:
    purpose: "provide guidelines, moderation, and support links"
```

## Layout

- Use balanced to dense content with visible activity signals.
- Show discussion cards in a grid or list depending on recency and reply depth.
- Keep joining action clear but not aggressive.

## Components

Use `HeroSection`, `DiscussionCard`, `MemberAvatar`, `ActivityFeed`, `ReactionButton`, `Tag`, `CTASection`, and `Footer`.

## Acceptance

- Users can understand community value before joining.
- Activity signals are legible and not color-only.
- Participation controls have accessible names and selected states.
