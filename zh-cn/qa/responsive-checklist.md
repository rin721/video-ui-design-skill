# Responsive Checklist

## Desktop

- Content fits within max container width.
- Grid columns maintain stable card dimensions.
- Utility navigation does not cover content.
- Side panels leave main content readable.
- Hover states do not shift layout.

## Tablet

- Grids reduce to 2 or 3 columns according to minimum card width.
- Side navigation collapses or remains readable.
- Tap areas meet minimum size.
- Forms and search remain usable.

## Mobile

- Navigation moves to top row, bottom bar, drawer, or sheet.
- Main content uses 1 or 2 columns only when cards remain readable.
- Text does not overflow buttons, cards, inputs, or labels.
- Media keeps stable aspect ratios.
- Modals and drawers fit within viewport height.
- No horizontal scroll.

## State Coverage

- Loading states preserve component dimensions.
- Error and empty states fit the same responsive shells.
- Focus indicators remain visible near viewport edges.
- Sticky elements do not obscure headings, inputs, or actions.
