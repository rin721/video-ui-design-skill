# Media Replacement Rules

## Safe Replacement

- Replace every image, illustration, logo-like mark, avatar, screenshot, and decorative motif with new material.
- Keep only generalized decisions: aspect ratio, density, placement, hierarchy role, and content purpose.
- Use original or licensed assets with documented usage rights.
- Use generated imagery only when it is suitable for the product, audience, and content task.
- Prefer real product, place, interface, state, or person imagery when users need inspection or trust.

## Do

- Define alt text intent before selecting media.
- Use stable aspect ratios for cards and grids.
- Provide placeholders for loading and missing media.
- Keep image contrast compatible with overlays.
- Crop consistently within a content group.

## Dont

- Do not reuse protected artwork, logos, screenshots, mascots, characters, or proprietary icons.
- Do not create visual marks that can be mistaken for a known brand.
- Do not rely on image text for essential meaning.
- Do not place busy media behind long text.
