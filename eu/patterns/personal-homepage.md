# Personal Homepage Pattern

## Structure

```yaml
personal_homepage:
  intro_hero:
    purpose: "establish name, role, and current focus"
  identity_statement:
    purpose: "explain what the person does and why it matters"
  selected_work:
    purpose: "show strongest projects or outcomes"
  writing_or_projects:
    purpose: "offer depth through posts, talks, experiments, or notes"
  contact_cta:
    purpose: "invite collaboration, hiring, speaking, or conversation"
  footer:
    purpose: "provide social links and secondary details"
```

## Layout

- Use balanced density with generous hero spacing and compact project cards.
- Keep avatar or portrait optional and replaceable.
- Use two-column work grids on tablet and desktop, one column on narrow screens.

## Components

Use `HeroSection`, `SectionHeader`, `ContentCard`, `MediaCard`, `AuthorBlock`, `CTASection`, and `Footer`.

## Acceptance

- Identity is clear without reading every detail.
- Selected work has role, outcome, and link.
- Contact path is visible near the end and optionally in navigation.
